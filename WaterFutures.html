Water Futures
Exhibition Installation

A design museum exists because of designers, and yet previous visitors to the Cooper Hewitt were rarely so; the idea of viewing the collection seeming more the realm of collectors than designers. With that in mind, I wanted to instead find a way to encourage museum visitors to look at the collection like designers—with a more opportunistic eye, and view the museum's permanent collections with renewed interest. Was there a way to motivate visitors to utilize the collection as designers rather than curate a design collection towards museum observers?

Hosted by A/D/O and exhibition curated by Jane Withers Studio
