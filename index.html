Quasi is a New York-based creative studio for experimental practice spanning the areas of design, art, technology, and interaction. The studio focuses on materials and digital/physical fabrication as a way to embody our rapidly changing relationship with technology. With a combined background in spatial and experiential design, computation, and hardware, Quasi strives to create provocative and engaging objects, installations and experiences.
